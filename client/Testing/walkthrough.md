
# **User Walkthrough Documentation**

## **Unified Internship & Mentorship Portal (UIMP)**

---

## **1. Overview**

The **Unified Internship & Mentorship Portal (UIMP)** is designed to help students find internships, connect with mentors, and manage learning progress. This walkthrough explains how users can use the portal step-by-step.

---

## **2. User Roles**

### **A. Student**

* Search and apply for internships
* Connect with mentors
* Track progress and tasks

### **B. Mentor**

* Create mentor profile
* Accept mentorship requests
* Guide students

---

## **3. User Walkthrough**

---

### **Step 1: Open UIMP Portal**

* Open the web browser
* Enter the UIMP URL
* The homepage will appear

---

### **Step 2: Registration**

1. Click **“Sign Up”**
2. Choose role: **Student / Mentor**
3. Enter details:

   * Full Name
   * Email
   * Password
   * College / Organization
4. Click **“Register”**

✅ After successful registration, the user is redirected to the dashboard.

---

### **Step 3: Login**

1. Click **“Login”**
2. Enter registered email and password
3. Click **“Login”**

✅ User will enter the dashboard.

---

### **Step 4: Dashboard Overview**

The dashboard shows:

* Recommended internships
* Mentorship requests
* Progress tracker
* Notifications

---

### **Step 5: Search Internships**

1. Click **“Internships”**
2. Use search bar or filters:

   * Domain (Web Dev, AI, Data Science)
   * Duration (1 month, 3 months)
   * Location
3. Click an internship to view details

---

### **Step 6: Apply for Internship**

1. Open internship details
2. Click **“Apply Now”**
3. Upload resume (optional)
4. Submit application

✅ Application is sent to the company.

---

### **Step 7: Find Mentor**

1. Click **“Mentorship”**
2. Browse mentor profiles
3. Click **“Request Mentor”**
4. Write a short message
5. Send request

---

### **Step 8: Mentor Accepts Request**

* Mentor receives notification
* Mentor accepts the request
* Student gets confirmation

---

### **Step 9: Communication**

* Use **Chat** feature (if available)
* Share tasks, doubts, and guidance
* Track progress

---

### **Step 10: Progress Tracking**

* Open **Progress Tracker**
* View tasks completed
* Check upcoming deadlines
* Add new tasks (optional)

---

## **4. Logout**

1. Click profile icon
2. Click **“Logout”**

---

## **5. End of Walkthrough**

This completes the basic walkthrough for UIMP.
Users can now successfully register, search internships, connect with mentors, and track progress.

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- **Browser**: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection required
- **JavaScript**: Must be enabled
- **Cookies**: Must be enabled for authentication

### First Time Access

1. **Visit the Platform**: Navigate to [https://uimp.com](https://uimp.com)
2. **Choose Your Path**: 
   - New user? Click "Sign Up"
   - Existing user? Click "Sign In"

## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

#### Step 1: Registration
1. Click the **"Sign Up"** button on the homepage
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Email Address**: Use your institutional or professional email
   - **Password**: Must be at least 8 characters with numbers and symbols
   - **Role**: Select your role (Student/Mentor/Admin)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for verification (if required)

#### Step 2: Profile Completion
After registration, you'll be prompted to complete your profile:

**For Students:**
- Full Name
- University/College
- Graduation Year
- Field of Study
- Skills and Interests
- Resume Upload (PDF format, max 5MB)

**For Mentors:**
- Full Name
- Company/Organization
- Job Title
- Years of Experience
- Areas of Expertise
- LinkedIn Profile (optional)

### Signing In

1. Click **"Sign In"** on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be redirected to your personalized dashboard

## Student Guide

### Dashboard Overview

When you log in as a student, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **Application Summary**: Quick stats of your applications
- **Recent Activity**: Latest updates on your applications
- **Upcoming Deadlines**: Important dates to remember
- **Mentor Feedback**: Recent feedback from your mentors
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to common tasks

### Managing Applications

#### Creating a New Application

1. **Navigate to Applications**:
   - Click "Applications" in the sidebar, or
   - Click "Add New Application" on the dashboard

2. **Fill Application Details**:
   ```
   Company Name: e.g., "Google"
   Position Title: e.g., "Software Engineering Intern"
   Application Platform: Choose from dropdown
   - LinkedIn
   - Company Website
   - Referral
   - Job Board
   - Career Fair
   - Other
   
   Status: Select current status
   - Draft (not yet applied)
   - Applied (application submitted)
   - Shortlisted (moved to next round)
   - Interview (interview scheduled/completed)
   - Offer (received job offer)
   - Rejected (application rejected)
   
   Application Deadline: Select date (optional)
   Resume: Upload your resume (PDF, max 5MB)
   Notes: Add any additional information
   ```

3. **Save Application**:
   - Click "Save as Draft" to save without submitting
   - Click "Save Application" to save and mark as applied

#### Viewing Your Applications

**Applications List View:**

- Filter by status, company, or date
- Sort by any column
- Search for specific applications

**Application Detail View:**
- Click on any application to see full details
- View all feedback from mentors
- See application timeline and status changes
- Download your submitted resume

#### Updating Applications

1. **Edit Application**:
   - Click the "Edit" button on any application
   - Update any field as needed
   - Click "Save Changes"

2. **Update Status**:
   - Change status as your application progresses

## Mentor Guide

### Dashboard Overview

As a mentor, your dashboard shows:

- **Student Overview**: Students you're mentoring
- **Recent Feedback**: Your recent feedback activity
- **Pending Reviews**: Applications waiting for your feedback
- **Analytics**: Your mentoring impact and statistics

### Managing Students

#### Viewing Assigned Students

1. **Students List**:
   - Navigate to "Students" in the sidebar
   - See all students assigned to you
   - View their basic information and progress

2. **Student Profiles**:
   - Click on any student to view their full profile
   - See their applications, progress, and history
   - Access their resume and contact information

### Providing Feedback

#### Creating Feedback

1. **Navigate to Student Applications**:
   - Go to "Students" ‚Üí Select a student ‚Üí View their applications
   - Or go to "Applications" to see all student applications

2. **Select Application**:
   - Click on the application you want to review
   - Review the application details and resume

3. **Write Feedback**:
   ```
   Content: Write detailed, constructive feedback
   - Be specific and actionable
   - Highlight both strengths and areas for improvement
   - Provide concrete suggestions
   
   Tags: Select relevant skill areas (required)
   - Resume: For resume-related feedback
   - DSA: For technical algorithm feedback
   - System Design: For system design feedback
   - Communication: For soft skills feedback
   
   Priority: Set feedback priority
   - High: Critical issues that need immediate attention
   - Medium: Important improvements to consider
   - Low: Nice-to-have suggestions
   ```

4. **Submit Feedback**:
   - Review your feedback for clarity
   - Click "Submit Feedback"
   - Student will be notified automatically

#### Feedback Best Practices

**Effective Feedback Structure:**
1. **Start Positive**: Acknowledge what the student did well
2. **Be Specific**: Point to exact areas for improvement
3. **Provide Examples**: Give concrete suggestions or examples
4. **End Encouraging**: Motivate continued improvement

**Example Good Feedback:**
```
"Your resume shows strong technical projects, particularly the e-commerce 
website with React and Node.js. The project descriptions are clear and 
quantify impact well.

For improvement, consider:
1. Adding more details about your role in team projects
2. Including specific technologies used (versions, frameworks)
3. Quantifying achievements where possible (e.g., 'improved performance by 30%')

Your technical foundation is solid - focus on better storytelling to 
highlight your contributions and impact."
```

#### Managing Feedback

**Edit Feedback:**
- Click "Edit" on any feedback you've given
- Update content, tags, or priority
- Changes are saved automatically

**Delete Feedback:**
- Click "Delete" if feedback was given in error
- Confirm deletion (this cannot be undone)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems

**"Invalid credentials" error:**
1. Double-check your email and password
2. Ensure Caps Lock is off
3. Try the "Forgot Password" link
4. Clear browser cache and cookies

**"Account not found" error:**
1. Verify you're using the correct email
2. Check if you need to sign up first
3. Contact support if you believe this is an error

#### Application Issues

**Can't upload resume:**
1. Check file format (must be PDF)
2. Ensure file size is under 5MB
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily

**Application not saving:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure all required fields are filled
3. Try refreshing the page and re-entering data
4. Contact support if the issue persists

#### Performance Issues

**Slow loading:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache
3. Disable unnecessary browser extensions
4. Try using a different browser

**Page not loading:**
1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. Check if the platform is under maintenance
3. Try accessing from a different device
4. Contact support if widespread issues

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is the platform free to use?**
A: Yes, UIMP is completely free for students and mentors.

**Q: Can I use the platform on mobile devices?**
A: Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Go to Profile ‚Üí Security Settings ‚Üí Change Password.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, contact support to request account deletion. Note that this action cannot be undone.

### Student Questions

**Q: How many applications can I track?**
A: There's no limit to the number of applications you can track.

**Q: Can I have multiple mentors?**
A: Yes, you can receive feedback from multiple mentors assigned to you.

**Q: What file formats are supported for resumes?**
A: Currently, only PDF format is supported for resume uploads.

**Q: How do I know when I receive new feedback?**
A: You'll receive email notifications and in-app notifications for new feedback.

### Mentor Questions

**Q: How many students can I mentor?**
A: The number depends on your availability and platform capacity. Contact admin for details.

**Q: Can I edit feedback after submitting it?**
A: Yes, you can edit or delete your feedback within a reasonable timeframe.

**Q: How do I track my mentoring impact?**
A: Use the analytics dashboard to view your mentoring statistics and student progress.

**Q: What should I do if a student isn't responsive?**
A: Contact the platform admin to discuss the situation and potential solutions.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, and Edge 90+ are fully supported.

**Q: Why am I getting logged out frequently?**
A: Sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity for security. You can adjust this in settings.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, you can export your applications and feedback data from your profile settings.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.
